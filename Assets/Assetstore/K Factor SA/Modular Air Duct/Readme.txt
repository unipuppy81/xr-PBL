Important:

The ducts have 2 sides, exterior and interior, their fitting measures (metric system) are square with 132 cm for the outside and 120 cm for the inside.

The models related to walls, floors and ceilings are only in the package to assist in the assembly of the scene, the ducts are the main product.

This package contains a script for fan rotation, enter a value between 300 and 500 (or other if you prefer) in the "speed" field in the inspector.
